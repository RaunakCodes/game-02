indRacer

This is a simple game developed by Raunak Raj.

Instructions For Playing: -

  • In this game there is car and road blocker.
  • You have to escape the car from the blockers. To gain score.
  • For moving your car: just click the side which side you want to move.
  • If your car crash with the blocker, Then your game is over.
  • For big screen user zoom the racing panel and use arrow key to move.
